WeeklyKnowledge offers an easy-to-read grid overview of your profession knowledge progress on all your characters.  
Quickly identify which points you've successfully gathered and where you might need to focus your efforts.  
Never lose track of your knowledge points with this addon.

Download WeeklyKnowledge today and take control of your gaming experience like never before!

## Slash Commands

- **/weeklyknowledge** - toggles the main window
- **/weeklyknowledge minimap** - toggles the minimap icon
- **/weeklyknowledge checklist** - toggles the checklist window

You can also use the short command **/wk**

## Bugs and Feedback

Your feedback is invaluable and instrumental in making WeeklyKnowledge the best it can be.  
If you come across any bugs or have suggestions for improvements, please don't hesitate to reach out.

To keep feedback in one place and make it much easier for me to fix bugs, ship improvements, and keep WeeklyKnowledge healthy long term, I am moving all support to the issue tracker instead of the comments. This helps make sure nothing gets lost, lets me track progress clearly, and gives your ideas a much better chance of turning into real features.

**Report bugs, suggest new features, or ask questions on the [WeeklyKnowledge GitHub repository](https://github.com/DennisRas/WeeklyKnowledge/issues)**
